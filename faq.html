Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing encompasses all online strategies used to promote brands, products, and services. It includes SEO, social media, email marketing, and more.

How can digital marketing benefit my business?

It helps increase brand awareness, attract potential customers, and boost revenue through targeted online campaigns.

Social Media Marketing

Which social media platforms should I use?

Your choice depends on your target audience. Instagram and TikTok are great for younger demographics, while LinkedIn is better for B2B marketing.

How often should I post on social media?

Consistency is key. Posting 3-5 times a week ensures visibility without overwhelming your audience.
